## ğŸ¯ The Problem: Why Odyssey?

Traditional safety systems are reactive. They identify the cause after an accident has already occurred. Project Odyssey aims to shift this paradigm from **post-incident analysis** to **pre-incident prevention**.

## ğŸ’¡ Our Core Solution: A Human-like Multimodal AI

Odyssey acts like an AI safety officer with superhuman senses, simultaneously **seeing**, **hearing**, and **understanding** the industrial environment.

- **ğŸ‘€ Vision:** Detects unsafe actions, access to hazardous zones, and equipment anomalies via CCTV.
- **ğŸ‘‚ Audio:** Identifies abnormal machine noises, alarms, and shouts through microphones.
- **ğŸ§  Contextual AI:** Fuses all discrete data points in real-time to understand the 'context' of a complex scenario, predicting potential accidents and issuing proactive alerts.
